Candied Bacon

Description

This is the recipe for Candied Bacon. The ingredients are very simple and all you need is an oven and a bowl! Try this delicious (but not the healthiest) recipe.

ingredients

  • Â¼ cup of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons of maple syrup
  • 1 pound bacon

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 130 degrees F
  2. Mix brown sugar, rice vinegar, maple syrup, black pepper in a bowl
  3. Place bacon on a cooling sheet and tray into the oven
  4. Heat for 10 mins, flip, heat for 5 mins
  5. Remove bacon and brush both sides with the mixture
  6. Heat bacon again for 5 mins
  7. Heat more unti bacon is brown and crisp
